MAHATMA GANDHI CENTRAL UNIVERSITY

Mahatma Gandhi Central University came into existence by an Act of Parliament, Central Universities (Amendment) Act 2014(No 35 of 2014). The University became functional on 3rd February 2016. MGCU is situated in Motihari (Bankat), on National Highway 28.
